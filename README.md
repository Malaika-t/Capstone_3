# Capstone_3 - EmporiUm Sales Performance
## Project Overview:
This Project analyzes sales performance for EmporiUm, a virtual student bookstore operating across college campuses. The report covers 4 years of data (2022–2025) for the Northeast region, which includes:

| State | Store Count | Sales Channel |
|-------|-------------|---------------|
| Maryland | 9 stores | Store + Online |
| Massachusetts | 17 stores | Store + Online |
| Maine | 6 stores | Store + Online |
| New Jersey | 16 stores | Store + Online |

## 📈 Key Insights

1. **Sales Growth:** NE region grew from ~$400K/month in 2022 to over $1M/month by late 2025 — nearly 3x growth in 4 years
2. **Technology Dominates:** Technology & Accessories drives ~70% of all NE revenue
3. **Massachusetts Leads Online:** MA accounts for nearly 48% of all NE online sales
4. **Line Chart - Spikes:** Clear revenue spikes every November–December across all 4 years
5. **Top General Book:** Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone (J.K. Rowling) is the top-selling fiction book in the NE region
6. **Maine Opportunity:** Maine contributes only 2.9% of total sales — potential opportunity for expansion
